About Me

My research focuses on the analysis of Finite Volume methods for the simulation of Stokes and Navier Stokes equations.
In particular, my research topics are DDFV method for the Navier Stokes problem with open boundary conditions and non-overlapping Domain Decomposition method. 

During my Post-doc, I will work on the development and analysis of numerical schemes for semiconductors models.
